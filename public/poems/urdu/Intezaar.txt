Intezar ke khayal se bhi ab dar lagta hai 
kya pata tu bhi kisi ke intezar me ho

Me to sab bayaan bhi kr chuka tujhse ab 
kya pata me bhi tere khayal me hun

Der to bohot ho gayi ab tera rah dekhte
kya pata tu koi mushkil sawal me ho

Jawab shayad tujhe bhi pata hai 
kya pata tu bhi kisi ke intezar me ho
